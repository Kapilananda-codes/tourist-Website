A City of Timeless Beauty

Kyoto, the former Imperial Capital of Japan, is a city that beautifully blends ancient tradition with modern life. With over a thousand temples, stunning gardens, and traditional teahouses, Kyoto offers a unique glimpse into Japan's rich cultural heritage. Walk through serene bamboo groves, spot geishas in the Gion district, and marvel at the golden splendor of Kinkaku-ji.

Whether you are seeking spiritual tranquility, culinary delights, or vibrant festivals, Kyoto captivates every visitor with its elegance and charm.

3-Day Sample Itinerary

Make the most of your trip with this plan.

Morning: Visit the Fushimi Inari Shrine.
Afternoon: Explore Kiyomizu-dera Temple and the surrounding Higashiyama District.
Evening: Wander through the Gion district, Kyoto's famous geisha district.

Morning: Walk through the Arashiyama Bamboo Grove.
Afternoon: Visit Kinkaku-ji (Golden Pavilion) and the nearby Ryoan-ji Temple with its famous rock garden.
Evening: Enjoy a relaxing dinner along the Kamo River.

Morning: Explore the Nishiki Market for local snacks and delicacies.
Afternoon: Participate in a traditional tea ceremony.
Evening: Last-minute souvenir shopping at Kyoto Station.
